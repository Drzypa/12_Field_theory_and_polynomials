polynomial long division


Given two polynomials a⁢(x) and b⁢(x) polynomial (long) division is a method for calculating a⁢(x)/b⁢(x) that is, finding the polynomials q⁢(x) and r⁢(x) such that a⁢(x)=b⁢(x)⁢q⁢(x)+r⁢(x).
